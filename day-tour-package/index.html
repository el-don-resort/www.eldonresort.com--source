Day Tour Package

Available 7 days a week between 8am to 5pm except if the resort has been rented for exclusive use. For the privacy of the in-house guests, the resort cannot accommodate tours beyond 5pm.

Day Tour Fees

Php 700 per adult

Php 350 per child (8 yrs old below)

0-2 years old Free of charge provided sharing plate with an adult

Inclusions

Plated Lunch with one round of iced tea Choices are:

  1. 1 pc Fried Chicken with Rice
  2. 1 pc grilled pork chop with rice
  3. 4 pcs Pork barbeque with rice
  4. 1 Pan grilled fish with rice
  5. Aglio Olio with garlic bread
  6. Bolognese with garlic bread
  7. Carbonara with Garlic bread
  8. Spaghetti Filipina with garlic bread

Other Inclusions

  1. Use of pool and beach area from 8am-5pm.
  2. Use of pool towel
  3. Day tour guests may use the shower and rest room area located near the pool.
  4. No wash- up room is included in the day tour package.
    • Every group of five, (1) table at the pool side will be assigned. Or may opt to occupy one (1) beach hut (Kubo) at the beach area for every group of 10 persons. Choose one area only.
    • Use of poolside tables and beach huts (kubos) are on a first-come, first-served basis. The resort has the right to assign which area the guests can occupy, based on availability.
    • We allow thirty (30) persons maximum day tour per day.
    • A maximum of twenty (20) persons will be allowed to occupy the poolside tables at a time.
    • A maximum of thirty (30) persons will be allowed to occupy the beach huts ( kubos) at a time.
    • Day tour guests will be assigned use of beach kubos if the pool cabana is occupied for a function.
    • Buffet service will require a minimum of 20 guests accumulated. The menu will be on chef’s choice for buffet which consists of: One Soup dish, One pasta/noodle dish/vegetable, One fish/ Seafood, One Chicken dish, One Pork dish, Rice and One Dessert.
    • During peak season, the resort will offer special buffet packages for lunch and dinner on chef’s choice menu for P 350/head .
    • Rates are subject to change without prior notice.
    • Bringing of outside food and drinks, coolers or cooking devices are not allowed. Cooking or any way of food preparation is not allowed. The resort has a restaurant and bar to cater to your requirements.

      • Drinks (softdrinks, beers, juices) - P 100/bottle or can
      • Chips/fruits - P 100/pack or container
    • All customers within the pool and beach area must be in swimming attire.
    • No one under the age of fourteen (14) should enter the pool and beach area without being accompanied by an adult who knows how to swim.
    • The pool may be closed and cleared periodically for a safety check or to apply chemicals, to adjust chemicals to ensure proper sanitation of swimming pool water.
    • Drunk and disorderly behavior will not be tolerated.

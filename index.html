At the Resort

Inspired by Mediterranean architectural design, El Don Resort offers aminities perfect for intimate events such as family reunions, weddings, seminars, and small group activities.

A swimming pool for children and adults is located in the middle of the property. Here at El Don, guests can also enjoy watersports activities like kayaking snorkeling and beach volleyball.

How to get here

For guests with private vehicles, El Don Resort is just a four-hour drive away from Davao City International Airport on a regular day time.

For guests who would like to take on public transportation, Governor Generoso bound vans are available at Paternos, Roxas Avenue in Davao City with first come first serve policy. Scheduled trips at 1PM and 10PM only.

Non-Air-conditioned buses bound to Pundaguitan or La Vigan are also available in Davao City Land Transport Terminal in Ecoland. Scheduled trips at 1:45AM, 3PM, 5PM and 10PM only.

    • Pictorial Package

    For a package of Php 6,000.00, a pictorial or photoshoot for weddings, debut or any occasion for families and group can be held at El Don Resort.


    Inclusions

    • One plated meal for a group of five; food options are from the day tour package
    • One wash up room available from 8 am-5 pm; and
    • Access to the resort’s beachline, pool area and resort grounds.
      • Pictorial schedule is from 8:00-5:00 PM only.
      • Excess of five persons will be paid Php 900 per head with one plated meal (food choices are from the day tour package) and access to the resort’s beachline, pool area and resort grounds.
      • Meal choices:
        Plated Meal with one round of iced tea Choices are:
        1. 1 pc Fried Chicken with Rice
        2. 1 pc grilled pork chop with rice
        3. 3 pcs pork barbeque with rice
        4. 1 pan grilled fish with rice
        5. Aglio olio with garlic bread or Bolognese with garlic bread.
      • If number of guests reach 20 pax, buffet service on Chef’s choice will be served.
      • Buffet consist of One Soup dish, One pasta/noodle dish/vegetable, One fish/seafood dish, One Chicken/Pork dish, Rice and One Dessert with one round of iced tea/juice.
      • Swimming at the pool or beach is allowed during the time allotted, from 8:00-5:00 PM.
